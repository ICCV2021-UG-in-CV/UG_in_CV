Undergraduates in Computer Vision

(Proposed ICCV 2021 Social)



Date: Between Oct 12-15, 2021 (1 day programme)

Time: EDT 13:30 - 17:40 (~6 hours)

  • Connecting undergraduates in theoretical & applied Computer Vision research worldwide, to mingle, collaborate & innovate.
  • Compile & share resources about breaking into CV research and industry as an undergraduate, via sharings from academia the industry and fresh graduates.

Schedule

13:35 - 14:05 Keynote 1
Professor Animesh Garg

  		Content & Format: AMA & sharing, Computer Vision research in academia (details tbc)

Animesh Garg is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at University of Toronto and a Faculty Member at the Vector Institute. He directs the UofT People, AI and Robotics (PAIR) group. He is affiliated with Mechanical and Industrial Engineering (courtesy) and UofT Robotics Institute. He is also a Sr. Research Scientist at Nvidia.

Content & Format: Speakers on the student panel will reflect on how they kickstarted their career in CV, their experiences researching as undergraduates, and share insights on postgraduate prospects.

14:45 - 15:15 Keynote 2
Dr. Eleni Triantafillou (tentative)

  		Content & Format: tbc

Dr. Triantafillou received her PhD from the University of Toronoto and is an incoming research scientist at Google Brain. Her main research interest is centered around the pursuit of intelligent systems that are flexible and adaptable, supporting the rapid acquisition of new knowledge using only a few examples, such as how meta-learning and episodic training can be leveraged to build good representations for few-shot learning.

Professor Zeynep Akata

  		Content & Format: tbc

Zeynep Akata is a professor of Computer Science (W3) within the Cluster of Excellence Machine Learning at the University of Tübingen. She received a Lise-Meitner Award for Excellent Women in Computer Science from Max Planck Society in 2014, a young scientist honour from the Werner-von-Siemens-Ring foundation in 2019 and an ERC-2019 Starting Grant from the European Commission.
